# Week 1 Launch Note Draft

CultureRadar is starting with a deliberately small universe: 15 US-listed companies across enterprise SaaS and semiconductors.

The first month is not about proving a grand thesis. It is about building a public record.

The operating promise is simple:

- No black-box scores.
- No buy/sell/hold calls.
- No retroactive edits to validation thresholds.
- Every published judgment gets a timestamp, source links, and a deadline.

## What Comes Next

The first internal pass will build baseline evidence logs for three companies:

- One possible improvement case
- One possible deterioration case
- One stable comparator

The first public card will only go out after the evidence log has all four lines: talent flow, employee review trend, management language, and external red flags.

## 中文摘要

CultureRadar 第一阶段只覆盖 15 家美股公司，集中在企业级 SaaS 和半导体。前三个月先免费公开运行，重点不是收费，而是建立一份可追踪、可证伪、不可事后改题的判断记录。

每一张公开卡片都会包含四条证据线、一个主验证指标、一个阈值和一个截止日期。

## Disclaimer

This is public-information research and investor education, not investment advice. CultureRadar does not provide buy, sell, hold, price-target, portfolio, or personalized recommendations. All judgments are timestamped observations with explicit validation criteria and may be wrong.
